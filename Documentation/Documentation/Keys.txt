ESC: Exit the program or close the current window.

F11: Toggle fullscreen mode.

SPACE: Pause or resume the simulation.

RIGHT ARROW: Increase the playback speed of the simulation.
LEFT ARROW: Decrease the playback speed of the simulation.

1: Set the playback speed of the simulation to 1.

Z: Toggle the visibility of dark matter in the simulation.
U: Toggle between density color and standard color for particles.

R: Restart the simulation from the beginning.

L: Toggle the camera focus between the origin point and free movement mode.

W: Move the camera forward.
A: Move the camera to the left.
S: Move the camera backward.
D: Move the camera to the right.
